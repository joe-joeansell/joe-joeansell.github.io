Media4Me - Figma Mobile App Prototype

2025 University Project. Figma prototype for a street-art focused image sharing and social media app.

Figma | Photoshop | Web Design | App Design | Graphic Design

The goal of this project was to create a working prototype for a functioning mobile app. It could be any kind of app, but I chose to go along with my artistic interests and create an image-sharing app which is focused around sharing graffiti and street art in your local area. I took inspiration from apps such as Snapchat, Instagram and Pokemon GO.

This project was developed entirely in Figma, using Components to recreate the features of a standard mobile application, such as scrolling, tapping and text entry. Elements such as colour schemes and logos were also designed by me, and most of the photography shown was mine as well.
