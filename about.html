About Me

My name is Joe, and I am a Creative Computing student with experience in a variety of disciplines, with particular enthusiasm for Web and Software work- I am well versed with HTML, CSS, Javascript and P5.JS. I am also quite familiar with the design software Figma, and have experience with video, audio and image editing.

Since 2023 I have been pursuing a Creative Computing Degree at the University of Portsmouth. I achieved First level work in my first year, and am on track to perform similarly in my second. I also previously worked as an IT assistant at YOURMeds in 2022, helping with upkeep on their web portal services.

Outside of my work as a student, I enjoy a variety of interests. I'm an avid gamer, and love playing and also learning how the games I enjoy were made. I also enjoy making art, reading fiction and non-fiction literature, and physical activity such as working out or paddleboarding.
