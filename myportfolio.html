Portfolio Website - Catalogue Of My Work

2025 Personal Project. A catalogue of my work from the past two years, mostly related to web and software development.

CSS | HTML | JavaScript | No Bootstrap

This website was developed by me to showcase my work. I developed the initial version over a period of about one week. It showcases a variety of my work over the past two years. Most of it is web and software related, though it is intended to display some of the other skills I have been developing, such as my ability to pitch and then bring a project to fruition, both whilst solo and whilst working with others.

I developed it mainly using HTML and CSS with some use of JavaScript where applicable, and coded mainly from scratch, borrowing on my previous web development and design experience. I did not use any frameworks such as Bootstrap.
